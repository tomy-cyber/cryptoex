Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a CryptoEx account?

To create an account, click the "Register" button in the top right corner of the page. Enter your email address, create a strong password, and verify your email. You can then complete identity verification (KYC) to unlock full trading features including higher withdrawal limits.

How do I enable two-factor authentication (2FA)?

Navigate to Account Settings > Security > Two-Factor Authentication. We support Google Authenticator, Authy, and hardware security keys (YubiKey). Scan the QR code with your authenticator app, enter the 6-digit code to confirm, and save your backup codes in a secure location.

What are the trading fees on CryptoEx?

CryptoEx uses a maker-taker fee model. Standard fees start at 0.10% for makers and 0.10% for takers. Fees decrease based on your 30-day trading volume and CRX token holdings. VIP users can enjoy fees as low as 0.02% maker and 0.04% taker. View the full fee schedule on our Fees page.

How long do deposits take to process?

Crypto deposits are credited after the required number of network confirmations: Bitcoin (2 confirmations, ~20 min), Ethereum (12 confirmations, ~3 min), and most other tokens within minutes. Fiat deposits via bank transfer take 1-3 business days, while credit/debit card deposits are instant.

What is the minimum withdrawal amount?

Minimum withdrawal amounts vary by asset. For Bitcoin, the minimum is 0.0005 BTC. For Ethereum, it's 0.01 ETH. For USDT, the minimum is 10 USDT. You can view the complete list of minimum withdrawal amounts and associated network fees on the Withdrawal page for each asset.

How do I reset my password?

Click "Log In" then "Forgot Password". Enter the email address associated with your account and we'll send a password reset link. For security, password resets disable withdrawals for 24 hours. If you no longer have access to your email, contact our support team with your identity verification documents.

What cryptocurrencies can I trade on CryptoEx?

CryptoEx supports 350+ cryptocurrencies and 800+ trading pairs. This includes major assets like Bitcoin (BTC), Ethereum (ETH), Solana (SOL), and Cardano (ADA), as well as a wide selection of DeFi tokens, layer-2 tokens, and newly listed projects. Check the Markets page for the full list.

Is my crypto insured on CryptoEx?

CryptoEx maintains the Secure Asset Fund for Users (SAFU), a $1 billion emergency insurance fund to protect user assets. 95% of all user funds are stored in cold wallets with multi-signature security. We also conduct regular proof-of-reserves audits published on-chain for full transparency.

How do I contact customer support?

You can reach our 24/7 support team through multiple channels: Live Chat (available on every page), email at support@cryptoex.com, or through our official social media channels. For urgent account issues, use the in-app support ticket system for priority handling. Average response time is under 5 minutes.
