Frequently Asked Questions

What happens if my collateral value drops?
If your collateral value falls below the maintenance margin, you will receive a margin call notification asking you to add more collateral or partially repay the loan. If the LTV ratio reaches the liquidation threshold, a portion of your collateral may be sold to bring the ratio back to a safe level.
What is LTV (Loan-to-Value) ratio?
LTV is the ratio between your loan amount and your collateral value. For example, if you deposit $10,000 in BTC and take a loan with 65% LTV, you can borrow up to $6,500. Lower LTV means lower risk and potentially better interest rates.
Can I repay my loan early?
Yes, you can repay your loan at any time with no early repayment penalties. Interest is calculated only for the period the loan was active. Your collateral is released immediately upon full repayment.
What currencies can I borrow?
You can borrow stablecoins including USDT, USDC, and BUSD. This allows you to access fiat-equivalent value while maintaining your crypto positions. Borrowed funds are deposited directly to your CryptoEx wallet.
Is there a credit check required?
No credit check is required. Crypto loans on CryptoEx are fully collateralized, meaning your deposited crypto serves as the guarantee. Approval is instant as long as you meet the minimum collateral requirements.
