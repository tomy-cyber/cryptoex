Frequently Asked Questions

What is an NFT?
An NFT (Non-Fungible Token) is a unique digital asset stored on a blockchain that represents ownership of a specific item such as art, music, video, or virtual real estate. Unlike cryptocurrencies, each NFT is one-of-a-kind and cannot be exchanged on a one-to-one basis.
How do I buy an NFT on CryptoEx?
To buy an NFT, create or log into your CryptoEx account, browse the marketplace, and click on any NFT you like. You can either purchase at the listed price or place a bid if it is an auction. Payment can be made using your CryptoEx wallet balance or a connected external wallet.
What are the fees for trading NFTs?
CryptoEx charges a flat 1% marketplace fee on all NFT transactions, one of the lowest in the industry. Additionally, creators may set royalty fees (typically 2.5-10%) that are automatically distributed on secondary sales.
Which blockchains are supported?
CryptoEx NFT marketplace supports multiple blockchains including Ethereum, BNB Chain, Polygon, Solana, and Arbitrum. Cross-chain bridging is available for seamless transfers between supported networks.
Can I create and sell my own NFTs?
Yes, CryptoEx offers a no-code minting tool that lets you create NFTs from your digital files. Upload your artwork, set your price and royalty percentage, choose your blockchain, and list it on the marketplace — all without any coding knowledge.
