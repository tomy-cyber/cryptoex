Frequently Asked Questions

What is Crypto Savings?
Crypto Savings allows you to deposit your idle cryptocurrency and earn passive interest over time. You can choose between flexible savings with instant withdrawals or fixed-term deposits with higher APY rates.
How is interest calculated?
Interest is calculated daily based on the amount deposited and the current APY rate. Earnings are distributed to your savings balance every 24 hours, and you can compound your interest automatically.
Can I withdraw from a fixed savings plan early?
Early withdrawal from fixed savings is possible but may result in forfeiture of earned interest. Flexible savings can be withdrawn at any time without penalty.
Are the APY rates guaranteed?
Fixed-term APY rates are locked for the duration of the deposit. Flexible savings rates are variable and may change based on market conditions and platform demand.
Is there a maximum deposit limit?
Each savings product has its own maximum deposit cap that varies by asset and term. You can view the current limits on each product card. Premium users may have higher deposit limits available.
